# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

The Roundtable community is dedicated to providing a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.
We expect all participants — contributors, maintainers, and users — to act
professionally and considerately in every project space.

## Our standard

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**, as its code of
conduct. The full text is published at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating, you agree to uphold it. In short: be kind, assume good faith,
welcome newcomers, accept constructive feedback gracefully, and focus on what is
best for the community. Disrespectful, harassing, or exclusionary behavior is
not tolerated.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull
requests, discussions — and whenever an individual is officially representing
the project in public.

## Reporting and enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
project maintainers. You can reach the Synesis Corp team through the
organization profile at https://github.com/Synesis-Corp. All reports will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the privacy and security of
the reporter will be respected.

> Maintainers: replace this with a dedicated contact address once available.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code and may take
any appropriate corrective action, up to and including a temporary or permanent
ban from the community, in response to behavior they deem inappropriate.
